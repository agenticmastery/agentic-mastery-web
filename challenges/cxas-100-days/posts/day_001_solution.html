Day 1 Solution: Console Navigation & Setup

👨‍🏫 Mentor Guidance

Here is the solution and verification for the Day 1 challenge. Since this was a setup day, success means you successfully navigated the console and saw the expected output in the Simulator.

The Challenge

Create an application named EcoShop-Sandbox, write a simple instruction, and test it in the Simulator.

The Recommended Solution

1. Application Creation

You should have a new application listed in your dashboard named EcoShop-Sandbox. If you click on it, you should see the Agent Workspace.

2. Instructions Verification

Your instructions in the workspace should look something like this (or the XML version if you jumped ahead!):

You are a helpful support agent for EcoShop, a brand selling eco-friendly products.
Be polite, enthusiastic, and always suggest recycling tips.

3. Expected Output in Simulator

When you typed *"Hi, what do you sell?"*, the agent should have responded with something like:

"Hello! Welcome to EcoShop. We sell a wide range of eco-friendly products to help you live a greener life! 

By the way, remember to recycle your old electronics to reduce waste! How can I help you today?"

What to check in the Execution Trace:

  • Verify that the model used the instructions you provided.
  • Confirm that no tools were called (since we haven't added any yet).
