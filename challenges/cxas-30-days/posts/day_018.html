Week 3 Detailed Summary

This week was all about ensuring your agent is safe, reliable, and fast. Here is a recap of the core topics we covered:

1. Offline Evals: Turn & Simulation

We learned how to evaluate agents before they go live. **Turn Evals** check the agent's response to a single input, while **Simulation Evals** test full multi-turn conversations (Customer User Journeys or CUJs) using Golden Datasets. This helps us catch logic errors, prompt drift, and hallucinations early.

2. Offline Evals: Tool & Callback (Isolation Testing)

We zoomed in to test individual components. By testing tools and callbacks in isolation (using mocks for external APIs), we can ensure they work as specified without the unpredictability of the LLM. This makes debugging much easier.

3. Guardrails Configuration

We explored how to keep the agent on track and safe. Guardrails prevent the agent from discussing off-topic subjects, generating harmful content, or making unauthorized commitments. They act as the safety net for the non-deterministic nature of LLMs.

4. PII Redaction & Data Loss Prevention (DLP)

Security is paramount. We looked at how to identify and redact Personally Identifiable Information (PII) like credit card numbers and addresses before data is logged or processed by external systems, ensuring compliance and user trust.

5. Online Evals & Latency

Finally, we learned how to monitor the agent in production. We discussed online evaluations of live traffic and the importance of monitoring latency metrics like **p50** (typical experience) and **p99** (worst-case scenarios) to ensure the agent remains responsive.

Pro-Tip: The Pre-Flight Checklist

Always create a "Pre-Flight Checklist" for every major agent update. This checklist should include running your Golden Datasets, verifying isolation tests for new tools, checking guardrail logs, and reviewing expected latency impact. Never rely on manual "smoke tests" alone.

Watch-Out: Over-Reliance on Offline Success

A common pitfall is assuming that a 100% pass rate on your offline Golden Dataset means your agent will be perfect in production. Real users are creative and unpredictable. Always complement strong offline evaluations with robust online monitoring and alerting.
